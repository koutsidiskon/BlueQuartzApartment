Privacy Policy

This Privacy Notice for Blue Quartz Apartment describes how and why we collect, store, use, and share your personal information when you use our services.

Summary of key points

  • We collect personal data you provide through inquiry and contact forms.
  • We use your data to answer inquiries and provide booking-related communication.
  • We do not intentionally process sensitive personal information.
  • You have GDPR rights such as access, correction, deletion, and objection.

1. What information do we collect?

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, such as:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Inquiry message
  • Check-in and check-out dates
  • Number of guests

2. How do we process your information?

We process your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Provide availability and booking communication
  • Protect the website and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Legal bases under GDPR

We process personal data on legal bases including consent, legitimate interests, contractual necessity, and legal obligations, depending on the context.

4. When and with whom do we share personal information?

We may share information with trusted service providers when necessary for hosting, communications, and lawful operations, or where required by law.

5. Cookies and tracking technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies for core functionality, analytics, and security. Where required, non-essential cookies should be governed by consent.

6. How long do we keep your information?

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes stated in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7. How do we keep your information safe?

We apply technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal information. No system can guarantee absolute security, but we use reasonable controls to minimize risk.

8. Do we collect information from minors?

We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18 years old. If such data is identified, we will take steps to remove it.

9. What are your privacy rights?

You may request:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction or objection to processing
  • Data portability where applicable

10. Controls for Do-Not-Track features

At this time, no universally accepted DNT standard exists. We may update this notice if legal standards change.

11. Updates to this notice

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect legal and operational changes. The revision date at the top indicates the latest version.
